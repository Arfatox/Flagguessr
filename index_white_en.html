Welcome to FlagGuessr

This Game is simply to find the name of the country corresponding to the flag that is displayed. Once your answer is entered, you can send it with the enter key or by clicking on the button provided for this purpose; it will display the name of the country whether your answer is correct or incorrect. And the background on which the answer is displayed indicates whether your answer is correct or not, green for correct and red for incorrect. There is a button to refresh the page and try it on a new country.
I'm proud to say that it is fully translated but if you spot any mistakes or have any questions contact me at : flagguessr@gmail.com
